About

App Selene

This application use artificial intelligence to detect psychological illnesses through a comprehensive questionnaire, evaluating various aspects of mental health and providing clear results. It integrates Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) techniques facilitated by licensed professionals, offering personalized tasks and exercises. Users can connect with appropriate healthcare professionals, access emergency emotional support, and use evidence-based solutions to enhance overall well-being. Additionally, the app features options to generate music based on psychological cases and listen to the Quran for spiritual support.
